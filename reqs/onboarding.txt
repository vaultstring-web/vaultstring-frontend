ONBOARDING WIZARD REQUIREMENTS
User Type Detection
Individual: Personal KYC information

Business: Company information + representative details

User Type Routing: Determine at signup or first login

Multi-Step Wizard Structure
For Individuals:

Personal Details: Full name, DOB, nationality, ID type

Contact Information: Phone, address (with country-specific formatting)

ID Verification: Document upload/photo capture (passport, driver's license, national ID)

Source of Funds: Employment status, income range (for AML compliance)

Security Setup: 2FA preference, security questions

Terms & Agreements: Regulatory disclosures, privacy policy

For Businesses:

Company Details: Legal name, registration number, tax ID

Business Information: Industry, size, website, incorporation date

Authorized Representatives: List of individuals with signing authority

UBO (Ultimate Beneficial Owner) Disclosure: Ownership structure

Banking Details: For payouts (account information)

Documentation: Certificate of incorporation, articles, shareholder register

Compliance: Business purpose, expected transaction volumes

Wizard Features:
Progress Indicator: Clear step tracking (breadcrumb or progress bar)

Save & Resume: Draft saving at each step

Validation: Per-step validation before proceeding

Back Navigation: Allow going back without losing data

Exit Points: Clear way to logout/save for later

KYC Compliance Notes:
Malawi Requirements: Align with RBM (Reserve Bank of Malawi) regulations

China Requirements: Consider PBOC (People's Bank of China) for merchant side

Documentation: Support multiple file types, size limits, preview functionality

Data Privacy: Clear indication of why information is collected
